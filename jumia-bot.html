One of the leading ecommerce companies in Africa, Jumia (formerly Africa Internet Group) is a network of over 120 websites across 23 countries that has raised almost $500m since its founding in 2012.

Chat Now

In order to make purchases from the huge assortment of options within the Jumia ecosystem easier, the Jumia management team decided to partner with LaunchCircle to build out an AI and Natural Language chatbot for Facebook that would allow users to quickly find the goods they were looking for.

Working closely with the Facebook Messenger team, LaunchCircle and Jumia developed an easy to use bot that allows users to see items, book hotels, contact support, and more, all while becoming more intelligent as more users utilize the chatbot.
